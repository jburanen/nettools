MQTT Client

Connect to an MQTT broker over WebSocket and browse published topics in real time. All processing runs in your browser — no data leaves your device.

// connection
Broker must have WebSocket support enabled. Common WebSocket ports: 8083, 9001, 8000. Path is /mqtt for Mosquitto, / for some others.
